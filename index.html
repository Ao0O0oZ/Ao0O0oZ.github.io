About Me

I received my M.S. in Bioinformatics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I also earned my B.S. in Biological Sciences. My academic honors include the National Scholarship, Outstanding Graduate of Higher Education Institutions in Anhui Province, and USTC Excellent Student Scholarships. My research focuses on autoimmune diseases, especially using single-cell multiomics, spatial transcriptomics, and integrative computational analysis to understand disease-associated immune cells and regulatory mechanisms.

Research Interests

  • Mining omics data to uncover autoimmune disease mechanisms
  • Integrating computational and biochemical approaches to study disease-associated regulatory mechanisms
