About Me

Ao Zheng was born in Shiyan, Hubei. He received his B.S. from the Shitsan Pai Talent Program in Life Sciences at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022, and his M.S. in Biology from USTC in 2026. During his master's study in Qu Lab, he worked on single-cell and spatial multi-omics studies of autoimmune diseases. He has published one high-impact paper as a co-first author and contributed to several research projects in multi-omics analysis. He has received honors including the National Scholarship, Outstanding Graduate of Higher Education Institutions in Anhui Province, and USTC Excellent Student Scholarships.

Research Interests

  • Mining omics data to uncover autoimmune disease mechanisms
  • Integrating computational and biochemical approaches to study disease-associated regulatory mechanisms
